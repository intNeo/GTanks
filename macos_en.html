How to play GTanks on macOS

Since most users use Windows OS, there was not much demand to port the client to another platform. This tutorial is about installing Adobe Flash Player and Flash client on Mac OS.
  1. You need to download two images .dmg install_flash_player_osx.dmg и flashplayer_32_sa_debug.dmg;
  2. Run "install_flash_player_osx.dmg";
  3. We agree to the license agreement and install;
  4. Check the third option “Never check for updates (not recommended)”;
  5. Right-click on "Flash Player" and click on "Extract only "Flash Player";
  6. Run "flashplayer_32_sa_debug.dmg";
  7. Hold down LMB and drag "Flash Player" to the "Desktop";
  8. We extract the image in the same way;
  9. Download the client. The downside of this client is that you will have to manually update it (that is, you need to download the archive again when a post about innovations comes out, so watch out!!!);
  10. Open "System Settings" and find the "Flash Player" item;
  11. Select the "Advanced" tab;
  12. Click on “Setting up a trusted folder...”;
  13. Click on “+” and select the path to the “client” folder;
  14. Check that the path to the folder has been added and then click on “Close”;
  15. Go to the "client" folder and launch "GTloader.swf" through "Flash Player";
  16. A warning will appear, click “Ok”;
  17. Ready!
I hope this tutorial helped you install the client on your Mac. Good luck on the battlefields!
